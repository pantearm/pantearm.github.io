As part of my activities as the initiator of the ‘ME’ charity group (Koodake Zamien Man) I taught at the library of ‘Koodakane Kar’, an organization which supports victims of child labor. The classes were meant to encourage learning through games and free discussions. Later on we worked with “Vahdat” organization for mentally disabled children and also the “Baghcheban” school for the hearing-impaired children.
