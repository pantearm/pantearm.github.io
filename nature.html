Nature:

All photos here are from Iran, mostly from the Alborz mountains and southern islands. Carrying my camera, not a compact one, makes my experience as a mountain-climber and nature explorer more difficult. The act of photography could go beyond the ordinary motivation to provide evidence of an experience or to elicit admiration from others, or to simply share one's presence. The process of photography, rather than the resulting photographs, can become a means of contemplation and discovery, and has been my main objective to take photographs in nature. Although, it's a certainty that it makes me delighted if these photos raise awareness towards the neglected Iran's nature.
