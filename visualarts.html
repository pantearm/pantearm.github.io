01. Photography
I have divided my photographs into different series to reflect my various interests and archive and document them in a meaningful way. None of these works were ‘aimed’ at the gallery however some were published in a magazine and displayed at a university gallery. All my photographs are taken by the Fe2 Nikon analogue camera and no effects, manipulation and editing process, whether analogue or digital, has been involved. They were all taken from 2010 to 2018.
